Meet Alva,
a radically new digital design tool built for cross-functional product teams.

Alva lets you to design interactive products based on the same components your developers are using for production websites. But our mission goes way further. And guess what – we are entirely open source.

Cross­functional single source of truth

Maintaining a separate design library next to the developer’s component library is quite weird. That’s why Alva lets you design with exactly the same code components your engineers use for the final application.

Instead of creating static shadow copies of your web component library for different design tools, We are starting with React and certain library requirements, but we plan to be platform-agnostic in the future.

Bringing web technology into web design

A website is more than a plain fixed-size drawing canvas, where you can position rectangles, circles and text whereever you want to. Responsive design doesn’t mean to have just three different viewports designed. And interaction is not only connecting to static screens with a transition. It can be way more.

Page elements

On the left side you all the components you are using on your page. You can rearrange them or nest them.

Responsive

Every viewport counts. You can easily adjust the preview viewport to test your design on every possible size.

Interactive

The web is interactive, so we should design interactively, too. Alva enables you to go beyond static screens.

The story behind Alva

»Alva« is the middle name of Thomas Alva Edison, who made electricity available to the public. And in Latin, »Alva« means Wisdom. We started Alva in August 2017 as an initiative at SinnerSchrader and since then a quite small, but very powerful product design and engineering team is working hard on building and evolving the Alva app and its ecosystem.
